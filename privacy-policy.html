TL;DR: We DO NOT collect, store, or share any personal information. Your data stays on your device.

1. Information Collection

TikTok TrendView does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. The extension operates entirely within your browser.

2. Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • TikTok API Keys: If you choose to enter API credentials, they are stored in your browser's local storage using Chrome's Storage API.
  • Extension Settings: User preferences are saved locally for functionality.

Important: This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The extension may interact with the following third-party services:

  • TikTok/Douyin: The extension accesses publicly available video data from pages you visit.
  • Download APIs: Free third-party APIs (e.g., tikwm.com) may be used for video downloads. Please refer to their privacy policies.

4. Permissions

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • Storage: To save your settings and API keys locally.
  • Downloads: To download videos to your device.
  • Host Permissions: To access TikTok and Douyin pages for displaying statistics.

5. Data Security

Since no data is transmitted or stored on external servers, the security of your information depends on your device's security. We recommend keeping your browser and device updated.

6. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all locally stored data by removing the extension
  • Clear API keys through the extension's settings
  • Stop using the Service at any time

9. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through GitHub Issues.
